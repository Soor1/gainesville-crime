Welcome to Gainesville Crime! The goal of this web app is to produce a heat map of the top areas of crime in Gainesville, while also exploring the differences in performance between computer science sorting algorithms. The two sorting algorithms being utilized will be heap sort and quick sort. You can input several different filters to specify what results will get produced. You can also toggle the SNAP, AED, and Blue Phone locations in the top left of the map. At the bottom of the custom input fields, you will see button for both heap sort and quick sort. Both will yield the same results in regards to the map, yet they will have difference in performance output. We will track this in terms of the number of operations each sort takes, and output this below each button.
